Beach Access
To find coastal access ways while on the road, look for the Coastal Access Logo symbol (the Coastal Commission's coastal access logo)that marks many (but not all) coastal access points. The California Constitution guarantees the public's right of access to tide lands, that is the area seaward of the mean high line. When visiting the coast, please observe any posted rules and please do not trespass on any adjacent private lands. Always be alert for sleeper waves and other hazards; stay safe and enjoy the coast.

Information Notice
Location and attribute data are collected primarily from entries in the statewide California Coastal Access Guide: Seventh Edition (2014), and the 4-book set of regional Experience the California Coast guides (2005, 2007, 2009, 2012), all published by the University of California, Press.
